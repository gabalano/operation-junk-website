FAQ

A few helpful answers.

How much does junk removal cost?

We provide the exact price after seeing the items, access, labor involved, and disposal needs in person. You approve the total before any work begins.

Can I schedule a pickup online?

Not directly. First, call, text, or schedule a callback so we can understand the job and personally coordinate the best removal time.

Can I choose a callback time?

Yes. Choose a preferred day and time window, and we will call you to discuss the job and confirm the next step.

Can you remove items from inside?

Yes. We can remove items from homes, garages, basements, upstairs rooms, apartments, storage units, and curbside areas. Access details may affect the final price.

Do you offer same-day pickup?

Same-day and next-day availability depends on the schedule, job size, truck capacity, and location. Call for the fastest answer.

What should I mention before we talk?

Please mention special items, stairs, elevators, long carries, tight access, dense debris, refrigerators, tires, mattresses, electronics, paint, or chemicals.
