FAQ

A few helpful answers.

Are the prices on the guide final?

No. They are typical starting ranges for standard household junk. Final price depends on volume, weight, access, labor, and disposal needs—and is confirmed onsite before work begins.

Do I have to send photos?

No. Photos are optional. They can help us understand the job and prepare, but you can start with a quick request, text, or callback instead.

Can I choose a callback time?

Yes. Choose a time window in the removal request. We will use it as your preferred callback time and follow up to confirm.

Can you remove items from inside?

Yes. We can remove items from homes, garages, basements, upstairs rooms, apartments, storage units, and curbside areas. Access details may affect price.

Do you offer same-day pickup?

Same-day and next-day service may be available depending on schedule, location, and job size. Request a pickup and we will tell you the soonest realistic option.

What should I mention before booking?

Please mention special items, stairs, elevators, long carries, tight access, dense debris, refrigerators, tires, mattresses, electronics, paint, or chemicals.
