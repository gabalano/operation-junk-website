FAQ

Common questions.

How much does junk removal cost?

Pricing depends on volume, weight, labor, stairs/access, and disposal requirements. Text photos for the fastest estimate.

Can I text photos for a quote?

Yes. Text photos to (872) 222-5502 and include your city, item details, and where the junk is located.

Do you offer same-day pickup?

Same-day and next-day pickup may be available depending on schedule, location, and job size.

Do I need to move items outside?

No. We can remove items from inside, garage, basement, upstairs, curbside, or storage units. Access details may affect pricing.

What items may cost extra?

Mattresses, tires, refrigerators, electronics, heavy materials, paint, chemicals, and special-disposal items may require extra fees or may be refused.

What items do you not take?

We do not take hazardous waste, active chemicals, asbestos, medical waste, flammables, explosives, or unsafe materials.
